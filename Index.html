🎨 Hashits Technology

Hashits (.ha files) are a proposed way to store color, texture, and light interaction as mathematical formulas instead of pixels. The goal: KB-sized files with photorealistic output.

🔴 RED SLASH COMPILER (Proposed Architecture)
├── Hashit Decoder - Reads .ha color math formulas
├── Depth Mapper - Positions colors in 3D space
├── Light Simulator - Calculates light interaction per pixel
├── Reality Assembler - Combines millions of hashits
└── Temporal Stabilizer - Target: 60+ FPS output

📍 Current Status:

This is a documented concept and my personal research direction. Implementation is in early stages. I'm sharing this to learn, get feedback, and find collaborators who share this interest. The vision is ambitious — the journey is just beginning.
